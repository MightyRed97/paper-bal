Abstract:
Hospital information systems in many institutions remain outdated, suffering from
poor interface design, limited portability and maintainability, weak security, and
insufficient information-sharing capability across departments. This paper proposes
a hospital integrated information management system built on a cloud computing
platform that realizes the optimized integration of hospital information resources,
improves the level of standardized management, and reduces cost. The cloud-based
design consolidates fragmented departmental systems, strengthens data security and
sharing, and provides a scalable and maintainable architecture for hospital
informatization, offering a reference model for modernizing hospital information
management.
